The Pixelated Miner

Built with GameMaker Studio

Download Project

Project Summary

The Pixelated Miner is a 2D video game programmed in GML, drawing inspiration from popular titles such as Terraria and Minecraft. Players must excavate and collect randomly generated ores scattered throughout the game's world map, with the objective of amassing 100 points within a 60-second time limit. The game's intuitive controls provide a user-friendly experience, while the use of procedurally generated ore deposits ensures a unique and dynamic gameplay experience with each playthrough

Features and functionalities

  • Torches: When a player places a torch, it emits light in a circular area around it, illuminating the surrounding area and making it easier to navigate.
  • ladders: Ladders are a versatile tool in The Pixelated Miner that allow players to climb up and down walls, making it easier to explore the underground caves.
  • Lighting system: As players move through the underground caves, the lighting shifts to reflect their position and create a sense of depth and atmosphere.
  • Random Resource Generation: The Pixelated Miner uses a sophisticated random resource generation system to create a dynamic and challenging underground world. In this system, resources are generated randomly throughout the game world, with the most common ores appearing near the surface and rarer resources appearing deeper underground.
